About

The Community of Developers (CODe) is the Students’ Association of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Christ College of Engineering, established in the year 2016. Various events like competitions, web and app development, workshops, etc are conducted by it. CODe proudly hosts its flagship event, South India’s one and only Beach Hackathon - Beach Hack. The Alumni continue to contribute to this forum by guiding their juniors on academics, placement and career. This association provides a space for students to develop their leadership & management skills, exhibit & share their technical skills. Overall, CODe brings together the students, alumni & staff of CSE Department as a single community.
